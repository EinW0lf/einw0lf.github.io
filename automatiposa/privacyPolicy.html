Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Automariposa (the "Bot") handles user data.

2. Data Collection and Use

  • The Bot does not collect any personal data from users.
  • The Bot stores usernames of players on the Wynncraft server to provide statistics. This data is collected without user interaction.
  • The Bot does not store or link any usernames to the Discord user who requested the data.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Bot does not share, sell, or disclose any user data with third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored by the Bot. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on the discord server. Your continued use of the Bot after any changes signifies your acceptance of the new policy.

7. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding these TOS, please contact me through discord by joining my server.
